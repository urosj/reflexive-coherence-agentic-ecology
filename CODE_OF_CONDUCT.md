# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a productive environment include:

- showing respect for different viewpoints and experiences;
- giving and receiving constructive feedback;
- focusing criticism on ideas, evidence, text, code, and documentation;
- asking for clarification before assuming bad intent.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, insults, or derogatory comments;
- public or private harassment;
- publishing others' private information without permission;
- unwelcome sexual attention or advances;
- trolling, personal attacks, or repeated bad-faith disruption;
- threats of violence or encouragement of harm.

## Scope

This Code applies in project spaces and when someone represents the project in
public spaces. Project spaces include issues, pull requests, discussions,
documentation, chats, and related events.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, text, code, issues,
and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code. They may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the repository owner
directly. Include what happened, where it happened, who was involved, and
whether you want the report kept confidential where possible.
