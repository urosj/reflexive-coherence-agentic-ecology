# AE01 Outcome and Stopping Contract

**Status:** revised and frozen at P1-I4 revision 0.24

**Preregistration version:** `1.1.0`

**Evidence effect:** no lane executed and no positive AE01 evidence opened

## 1. Purpose and authority

This contract defines how every AE01 hypothesis can terminate before any lane
result is observed. It is read together with:

- the P1-I3 common meaning and machine-shape contract;
- the seven lane hypotheses;
- the cross-lane synthesis hypotheses; and
- the P1-I4 control and failure register; and
- the developmental interpretation contract.

P1-I4 adds preregistered interpretation rules and stable hypothesis, control,
and failure IDs. Revision 0.24 reopens P1-I3 explicitly and adds first-class
metric-sheet, metric-calibration, and developmental-interpretation records at
schema `1.1.0`. Terminal labels carry synthesis status; they do not replace the
metric relation, boundary ladder, support status, or two-axis interpretation.

## 2. Meaning of hypothesis support

`Supported` means only that retained AE01 evidence satisfies the frozen
signatures for the named hypothesis within its lane ceiling. It does not mean:

```text
native LGRC support
catalog admission
transferability
successful composition
motif or regime evidence
semantic communication or coordination
agency, organism, life, or ecology proof
selection of a graph-side N31+ experiment
```

Inherited graph evidence, ecology interpretation, constructed mechanism,
missing surface, proposed discriminator, and observed AE01 result remain
separate evidence roles. A constructed contrast may support a demand or
missing-surface hypothesis but cannot support a native-surface claim.

## 3. Finite outcome vocabulary

Every lane must end in exactly one terminal classification:

| Classification | Required interpretation | Scientific effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `supported_bounded_candidate` | Every mandatory support signature and control passes within the lane ceiling; its interpretation still distinguishes robust, narrow, resolution-limited risk, support dependence, and blocked higher rungs. | Supports the bounded lane hypothesis only at its recorded boundary rung and claim ceiling. |
| `partial_or_mixed` | At least one required leg or adjacent property is supported, but another required leg is unstable, narrow, mixed, producer-carried, fixture-bound, interferes, or remains debt-limited. | Preserves every reached rung and records every downgrade and unexpected class. |
| `not_supported` | A complete valid comparison does not support the named hypothesis or satisfies its frozen null, with adequate controls and verified reconstruction. | Negative scientific evidence for the bounded hypothesis; lower, adjacent, counter-directional, or unexpected properties remain available for classification. |
| `blocked_missing_prerequisite` | A required surface, capability, carrier, discriminator, or bounded construction cannot be provided, and the missing prerequisite is completely classified. | Demand or missing-surface evidence only; neither support nor refutation. |
| `incomplete_execution` | The registered comparison cannot complete or cannot produce verified retained evidence within its attempt budget. | Neither support nor refutation; forces N31+ non-selection. |

`Negative`, `blocked`, and `incomplete` are not synonyms. Runtime
unavailability, validation failure, or exhausted reconstruction is never a
scientific null.

The terminal vocabulary is deliberately finite for synthesis. The referenced
developmental interpretation is deliberately richer. Neither can replace the
other.

## 4. Required comparison groups

P1-I5 must materialize a finite comparison matrix for each lane. Every matrix
contains these logical groups unless the lane contract explicitly marks one
inapplicable with a rationale:

| Group | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| `reference` | Establish the declared participant, medium, parent context, budgets, and baseline response. |
| `candidate` | Exercise the exact medium-dependent relation named by the lane. |
| `withdrawal` | Remove or disable the candidate mechanism or constructed scaffold. |
| `active_null` | Preserve nearby activity while breaking the proposed causal relation. |
| `lineage_or_source` | Break, shuffle, or substitute attributable source lineage. |
| `budget_or_leakage` | Expose free trace, memory, movement, maintenance, or hidden transfer. |
| `transfer_or_contrast` | Apply one frozen meaningful variant or record explicit transfer debt. |

Lane hypotheses may require additional comparison groups. P1-I5 chooses exact
fixtures, deterministic seed sets, metric thresholds, run limits, and resource
budgets, but may not remove a P1-I4 group or change its causal purpose.

## 5. Bounded attempt rule

Before P1-GATE, P1-I5 must freeze for every comparison cell:

```text
configuration identity
finite deterministic seed set or explicit deterministic no-seed declaration
maximum attempts
timeout and resource envelope
required artifacts and verification command
success, invalid-run, and infrastructure-failure criteria
```

The attempt budget is the frozen matrix, not “run until the desired result is
clear.” An invalid cell may be rerun only for a preregistered infrastructure
reason and remains visible. A scientifically valid narrow, mixed, counter, or
unexpected result may motivate a new bounded refinement or alternative, but
that is a new preregistration rather than a rerun. Extending seeds, thresholds,
variants, attempts, or time after observing an outcome is a contract revision
requiring affected cells and dependent synthesis to rerun.

## 6. Terminal decision procedure

After the frozen attempt matrix is exhausted or a prerequisite prevents it
from starting, first create the developmental interpretation: derive every
metric relation, classify every boundary rung, preserve expected/adjacent/
unexpected properties, record both axis readings, and declare a bounded next
move. Then apply the first satisfied terminal rule:

1. If required execution or reconstruction did not complete, classify
   `incomplete_execution` and record attempted work, failure, and missing
   information.
2. If execution could not begin because a declared prerequisite is absent or
   unsuitable, and that absence has a complete demand/discriminator record,
   classify `blocked_missing_prerequisite`.
3. If every mandatory comparison is valid and all support signatures and
   controls pass, classify `supported_bounded_candidate`.
4. If valid evidence supports only a strict subset, or a downgrade failure is
   present, classify `partial_or_mixed`.
5. If the valid completed probe satisfies its null or fails every required
   support path, classify `not_supported`.

Claim inflation is corrected before classification. A weaker valid result may
survive at a lower ceiling even when an unsafe label is rejected.

No threshold relation directly selects a terminal class. It constrains the
machine facts that the rung, signature, control, and support interpretation
must explain.

## 7. Stopping evidence

A lane stops only when its terminal record and controlling preregistration
include:

- the lane ID, which resolves one-to-one to the frozen lane hypothesis ID;
- comparison cells attempted and their terminal statuses;
- positive, negative, blocked, and missing signatures;
- mandatory common and lane-specific control outcomes;
- failure classifications and debt dispositions;
- retained evidence and verified reconstruction references;
- execution and realization receipts where live execution was requested;
- metric-sheet and candidate-blind calibration references;
- the developmental interpretation, highest valid rung, support status,
  threshold relation, blocked claims, and next-move falsifier;
- claim impact and blocked relabels; and
- the exact stopping rule used.

An open-ended “more investigation needed” result is invalid. Missing
information must be converted into a bounded next discriminator, a blocked
prerequisite, or an incomplete-execution record.

## 8. Cross-lane boundary

Lanes execute independently and do not consume another lane's conclusion.
Cross-lane recurrence is evaluated only after all seven terminal records exist.
A failure remains an atlas row and catalog input; it is not removed to improve
recurrence or ranking.

## 9. P1-I4 boundary

This contract freezes outcome meaning and stopping logic only. It neither
assigns a terminal result nor establishes that any required mechanism exists.
Review R2 assigned `AE01-C2` for the complete Phase 1 contract, not for a lane
result. No terminal result or higher scientific rung is assigned.
