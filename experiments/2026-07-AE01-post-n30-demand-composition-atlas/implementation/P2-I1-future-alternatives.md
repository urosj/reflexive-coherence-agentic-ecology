This record preserves alternatives considered after the successful C02
closeout so they can be recovered if later evidence makes them useful. It is

change-ledger entry, or promise of future execution.

Recording an alternative here does not:

- create a successor to C02;
- authorize calibration, registration, implementation, or runtime work;
- upgrade C02 beyond its bounded constructed claim;
- let another lane consume C02 as its scientific evidence; or

Any future activation requires a new question and falsifier, ownership review,
candidate-blind preregistration, separately frozen cycle, and evidence records.
C02 must remain unchanged.

## 2. Why alternatives were considered

C02 established one internally coherent realization of the relation:

```text
participant-attributable history
  -> ordered pulse-contact and feedback medium
  -> selective later native opportunity formation
```

It did not establish that the same relation recurs under a different medium
structure or under materially different temporal and access decisions. Two
different follow-up questions therefore remain conceivable:

1. **structural recurrence:** does the relation survive a change in the
   mechanism that carries history?; and
2. **decision robustness:** does the same mechanism remain informative when
   opportunity and access policies change?

Structural recurrence would be convergent evidence across realizations.
Decision robustness would be local evidence against fixture or orchestration
dependence. Neither is required for the bounded C02 result.

## 3. `P2-I1-HIST-ALT-STRUCT-001` — topological or basin-carried history

### Historical proposal

Revisit the child-basin/topology family deferred by `P2-I1-DEC-001` Option C:

```text
participant history
  -> local basin, boundary, or route geometry changes
  -> the changed state remains non-private and locally accessible
  -> a later route or local differentiation forms selectively
```

Unlike C02's ordered surface ledger, this realization would ask whether
history can be carried by a spatial or topological state. A comparable result
could show that the operational niche-conditioning relation is not peculiar to
the feedback-ledger carrier.

### Ownership and risk

This proposal overlaps the causal discriminators of:

- L05, maintained boundary exchange; and
- L07, parent-basin modulation or a missing graph distinction.

It must not be introduced as an L01 variant if its boundary or parent/local
distinction actually carries the result. Later lane work or synthesis should
decide ownership before any preregistration. A positive result would remain a
new bounded result; it would not retroactively naturalize C02.

execution_authority = none

## 5. `P2-I1-HIST-ALT-DEC-001` — continuous-history shared access

### Historical proposal

Keep the C02 feedback-conditioned realization, medium definition, response
family, causal controls, and claim discipline, but revisit a coherent subset
of accepted decisions:

- replace the independent counterfactual branch policy in `P2-I1-DEC-006`
  with ordered access by distinct later readers in one continuous history;
- replace the minimal one-read timing in `P2-I1-DEC-009` with a separately
  bounded repeated- or delayed-access window;
- replace the carrier-load axis in `P2-I1-DEC-018` with a declared access,
  delay, or persistence axis; and
- retain opportunity-level outcomes so depletion, idempotency, reinforcement,
  interference, and medium survival cannot disappear inside one fraction.

This would test a limitation deliberately left outside C02. C02 showed that
multiple reader configurations can access the same frozen history in
counterfactual branches. It did not show multiple accesses unfolding through
one continuous ecology.

### Interpretive value

A positive result could support bounded sequential shared access. A negative
or mixed result could expose single-use idempotency, depletion, reader
interference, missing persistence, or a missing shared-access substrate. Those
are informative classifications rather than reasons to patch until success.

Because source-current duplicate suppression and the absence of a natural
decay law may dominate the result, the exact variant should be selected only
after a future question identifies which relation is actually discriminating.

### Historical disposition

```text

priority = highest_same_structure_alternative
execution_authority = none
```
