## 1. Role and authority

This brief supplies the semantic and observational center for P2-I1. It asks
what minimal shared-medium niche formation could mean geometrically and
ecologically, what the lane should distinguish, which questions must remain
open, and when those questions may be answered.

It is subordinate to the frozen Phase 1 contracts. It may make their meaning
more explicit for L01, but it cannot change the hypothesis, comparison groups,
controls, metric relation vocabulary, boundary ladder, terminal states, or
claim ceiling. A conflict fails closed and requires the controlling contract
to reopen.

The brief is intentionally not a result, registration record, realization
profile, or execution report. It prevents implementation convenience from
quietly becoming the theory of the lane.

## 3. Core question

P2-I1 asks:

> Can an attributable history written into a declared non-private medium alter
> the later possibility, persistence, re-entry, cost, or susceptibility of a
> local differentiation within a declared support context?

The important object is not merely a trace or changed value. It is the causal
relation between medium history and a later local possibility.

The question remains valid when the result is positive, narrow, mixed,
counter-directional, null, blocked, or incomplete. A failed niche
interpretation may still expose a useful medium, measurement, construction, or
missing-surface class.

“Niche formation” names the search direction inherited by the lane title. The
highest L01 claim remains a bounded niche-conditioning relation and its LGRC
demand implications; full ecological niche formation is not assumed.

## 4. Ecology meaning

L01 keeps three objects distinct:

```text
niche medium state:
  a historically changed, non-private substrate condition

niche-conditioning relation:
  the participant-relative effect of that condition on later formation,
  persistence, re-entry, cost, or susceptibility

niche formation:
  attributable participant activity creates or modifies that relation
```

For L01, a minimal shared-medium niche is a participant-relative,
history-conditioned possibility structure carried by a non-private medium. A
niche-conditioning relation is supported when attributable medium history
differentially changes the later formation, persistence, re-entry, cost, or
susceptibility of a declared local differentiation under intervention and
matched controls.

A minimal niche is not reducible to:

- spatial location alone;
- passive surroundings around a pre-given agent alone;
- a mailbox, database, label, role, resource counter, or global controller
  alone;
- proof of population ecology, organism identity, agency, or regime; or
- an earlier/later correlation without attributable medium history.

A niche may have spatial, resource, boundary, support, and later semantic
components. None of those components alone establishes the conditioning
relation.

`Local differentiation` means the exact registered local carrier whose
formation or continuation is measured: for example a basin candidate, boundary
expression, route continuation, or another bounded carrier with an explicit
continuity criterion. The term does not pre-assign agent or organism status.

This meaning is relational:

```text
no participant without a condition of possible differentiation
no relevant medium without possible local response
no niche claim without attributable history-dependent conditioning
```

The participant and medium need not already be fully naturalized. They must,
however, be separated operationally or related through a non-circular
co-constitution account. L01 tests historically produced niche conditioning.
A pre-existing condition that affects later persistence without attributable
participant writing may support occupancy or environmental suitability, but
not the L01 formation direction. L01 does not attempt to define all ecological
niches or every case of participant-environment compatibility.

If producer state supplies participant identity, medium history, and later
response together, the result remains producer-carried rather than niche-
conditioned.

## 5. Geometric meaning

The minimal geometric proposition is:

```text
local activity at time t
    -> attributable perturbation of non-private carrier M
    -> bounded persistence or decay of changed M
    -> later local opportunity at t + tau
    -> changed formation, persistence, re-entry, cost, or susceptibility
```

The changed medium may be expressed through substrate-carried or explicitly
constructed geometry such as support distribution, flux affordance, route
cost, trace, event history, boundary condition, or susceptibility surface.
Substrate-carried expression is preferred but not assumed available. A
constructed surface remains admissible only with explicit necessity,
minimality, withdrawal, producer, naturalization-debt, and claim boundaries.

For the relation to be geometrically meaningful:

1. The medium change must exist outside private participant state.
2. Its source and history must be auditable.
3. It must persist or decay over a declared window rather than remain an
   instantaneous producer handoff.
4. A later response must depend on the changed condition under intervention.
5. Nearby activity, trace quantity, parent context, support, cost, and producer
   paths must be controlled or shown not to explain the result more directly.
6. Parent and support contexts must be declared and controlled rather than
   acting as an unnamed medium that carries the effect.

### 5.1 Selectivity gate

A uniform improvement or degradation may support generic environmental
conditioning. The niche interpretation additionally requires a declared
participant-relative, compatibility-relative, location-relative, or
formation-relative discriminator:

```text
same or comparable medium history
+ different registered compatibility, participant state, location, or class
-> different later admissibility, susceptibility, or cost
```

This does not require multiple species, agents, or simultaneous participants.
It requires a differentiated possibility structure rather than a globally
better or worse environment. The susceptibility-inversion cell is the primary
frozen discriminator, supplemented only by the registered carrier/timescale
contrast.

A niche-conditioning claim concerns a changed distribution or admissibility of
later possibilities. One realized formation, re-entry, or failure is an event,
not by itself a possibility-space change.

### 5.2 Co-constitution gate

A co-constitution account is admissible only when participant and medium have
separable causal roles under intervention, even if they are not ontologically
independent components. At minimum:

- medium state is reconstructible independently of the participant label;
- participant continuity is not inferred solely from the medium outcome;
- writing and later encounter have temporal or intervention separation;
- medium ablation changes the later response; and
- participant ablation and medium ablation have distinguishable effects.

Otherwise participant, medium, and response may be three names for one
producer-carried state.

### 5.3 Sharedness gate

The medium is “shared” because it is not exhausted by one participant's private
state and is accessible in principle to a later local differentiation other
than the writing event itself.

| Surface relation | Classification |
| --- | --- |
| Alters only the originating participant's private state | Self-aftereffect; not a shared-medium result |
| Exists outside private state and can condition a later local differentiation | L01 shared-medium candidate |
| Receives causally relevant contributions from several sources | Potential L02 shared-pool candidate |

P2-I1 does not require simultaneous multi-participant coordination. Multiple
attributable co-contributors, especially when contribution structure matters,
are the stronger defining question of L02.

## 6. Observation model

The frozen primary metric is a paired normalized margin:

```text
candidate later-response value - matched comparator later-response value
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
max(abs(reference response), abs(comparator response), measurement resolution)
```

The exact later-response surface must be selected before candidate execution
from the frozen family:

```text
formation | persistence | re-entry | cost | susceptibility
```

These are mutually exclusive choices for the primary response. Selecting
several after observing which one looks strongest is prohibited. Any other
response remains an explicitly secondary observation with its own claim
boundary.

The registration bundle must freeze an orientation transform before candidate
outcomes exist:

```text
oriented response =  raw response   when higher is the aligned direction
oriented response = -raw response   when lower is the aligned direction
```

The metric sheet then consumes oriented candidate and comparator response
values, preserving its frozen `greater_than_zero_is_aligned` rule. This is a
concrete operationalization of the existing sheet, not a change to its
threshold or relation vocabulary. Magnitudes from different response families
are not directly comparable.

Per-seed margins remain visible. The strict zero threshold and candidate-blind
resolution band describe direction and resolution; they do not decide the
terminal class. A robust margin can still be producer-carried or proxy-like. A
narrow, mixed, or counter-directional relation may still disclose a causal
boundary or a better next question.

The claim is distributional: seeds and registered opportunities estimate how
the possibility structure changed. No single realized event can satisfy the
niche-conditioning interpretation.

The maximum L01 claim remains the frozen **bounded niche-conditioning demand
pattern**. Rung `AE01-L01-R05` does not make the support native. A maximum-rung
result may still be scaffold-dependent, narrow, costly, fixture-bound in
another respect, or ineligible for reuse.

For this lane:

```text
bounded niche-conditioning relation candidate:
  the controlled ecology-side relation observed within the registered scope

bounded niche-conditioning demand pattern:
  that relation plus the explicit substrate distinction, support, or reusable
  mechanism that later LGRC work would need to carry more natively
```

The observed relation and its LGRC demand implications remain separate fields
of interpretation even when both are retained in the same lane report.

## 9. Anti-subsumption boundary

L01 supplies a general history-conditioning question. It must not absorb a
more specific lane merely because that lane also changes later continuation.

| Other lane | Defining causal distinction | When an L01 observation should redirect rather than subsume |
| --- | --- | --- |
| L02 — shared-pool co-conditioning | Multiple attributable sources jointly condition one auditable pool | Source plurality, interaction, order, or contribution structure—not simple additive repetition of L01 writing—carries the result. |
| L03 — trail/stigmergic field | Costly deposition and persistent trace alter later route choice or continuation | Route induction, reinforcement, or false-trace behavior is the primary dependent function. |
| L04 — nursery/support field | Generative or redistributive support alters fragile identity admissibility | Provisioning, subsidy, fragility, or support withdrawal is the causal center. |
| L05 — boundary-conditioned exchange | Maintained selective coupling controls ingress, egress, transformation, leakage, or repair | Interface maintenance and permeability carry the effect. |
| L06 — capacity circulation | Audited capacity depletes, replenishes, leaks, moves, or maintains floors | Ledgered circulation rather than general conditioning explains eligibility. |
| L07 — parent-basin missing surface | Parent/local current distinction is required but absent or unsuitable | The result depends on a graph distinction P2-I1 cannot instantiate honestly. |

A redirect does not erase the L01 observation or move its evidence into another
lane. It becomes a developmental next move or an input to later P2-I8
synthesis, with independent lineage preserved.

When a more specific lane's causal discriminator explains the result, that
lane owns the stronger interpretation. L01 may retain the general medium-
history observation but must not subsume the specific function.

## 13. Result interpretation

Every completed result is read along both axes:

**Becoming reading:** What medium history appeared? What later difference
appeared? At which rung? Under which support? Which stronger claim remains
blocked?

**Development reading:** What condition produced or prevented the relation?
Should the next move vary, withdraw, substitute, redesign, redirect, redescribe,
or stop?

The result must preserve:

- every per-seed margin and threshold relation;
- all five boundary-rung dispositions;
- expected, adjacent, and unexpected properties;
- support status and T0–T4 classification value;
- control outcomes and applicability dispositions through retained evidence;
- strongest valid claim and blocked relabels; and
- one next move with a falsifier.

No optimization may target the margin itself. A local refinement must target
the disclosed function and become a new preregistration under the D-038 guard.

## 15. Exit boundary

P2-I1 closes only when the frozen attempt matrix has one complete terminal
classification, every mandatory control outcome resolves, retained evidence
reconstructs, and the developmental interpretation records the lowest honest
boundary and a bounded next move.

The maximum handoff is a bounded niche-conditioning demand pattern with its
support, transfer, debt, and claim boundaries intact. P2-I1 cannot establish a
general niche theory, native shared-medium organization, communication,
coordination, agency, organism identity, motif, regime, life, or N31+
selection.
