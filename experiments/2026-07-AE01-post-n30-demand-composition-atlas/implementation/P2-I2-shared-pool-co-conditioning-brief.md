## 1. Role and authority

This brief supplies the semantic and observational center for P2-I2. It asks
what shared-pool co-conditioning could mean geometrically and ecologically,
what relation must distinguish it from repeated P2-I1-style writing, which
questions must remain open, and when those questions may be answered.

It is subordinate to the frozen Phase 1 contracts and the prospective post-R3
amendment. It may make their meaning more explicit for L02, but it cannot
change the hypothesis, comparison groups, controls, metric relation
vocabulary, boundary ladder, terminal states, or claim ceiling. A conflict
fails closed and requires the controlling contract to reopen before
calibration.

The brief is intentionally not a result, calibration record, registration
record, realization profile, execution plan, or implementation selection. It
prevents the first convenient multi-source representation from quietly
becoming the theory of a shared pool.

Review R3 also constrains how the lane should be implemented. P2-I2 may reuse
the common contract shape and evidence methods proven by P2-I1, but it should
not mechanically reproduce P2-I1's bespoke ceremony. It must keep one
evidence-expandable checklist, one cumulative lane decision record, compact

## 3. Core question

P2-I2 asks:

> Can distinguishable perturbations from at least two attributable sources
> jointly constitute one auditable non-private pool state or history so that a
> later continuation depends on the combined shared state rather than on
> private partitions, independent writes, direct messages, contributor labels,
> or a global controller?

The important object is not merely the presence of two records or two
successful source events. It is the causal relation between multiple
contributions, one functional carrier, and a later response to the combined
state or history.

Non-linearity is not required. An additive realization can qualify if the
addition occurs in one declared carrier, both source lineages remain auditable,
the resulting state has one functional identity, and matched controls show
that later continuation depends on that combined state rather than on two
separately consulted source records or a central sum. Conversely, a nonlinear
score produced by a hidden controller does not establish a pool.

The question remains valid when the result is positive, narrow, mixed,
counter-directional, null, blocked, or incomplete. Failure to find an adequate
native pool may still expose a precise missing surface, an unsuitable current
abstraction, or a useful constructed discriminator for later LGRC work.

“Pool” names the ecological search direction. It does not pre-assign resource,
collective-memory, communication, cooperation, or coordination semantics.

## 4. Ecology meaning

L02 keeps four objects distinct:

```text
shared carrier:
  one non-private substrate or explicitly constructed medium surface whose
  access is not exhausted by a private sender-receiver path

attributable contribution:
  a source-bounded perturbation whose lineage, order, amount or condition,
  and cost/support context remain auditable

combined pool state or history:
  one reconstructible carrier condition jointly constituted by at least two
  distinguishable contributions

co-conditioning relation:
  a later continuation whose response depends on that combined pool state or
  history under contributor, shuffle, freeze, and controller controls
```

A shared-pool co-conditioning relation is supported when the registered later
response depends on a jointly constituted carrier condition and the complete
control matrix rejects simpler explanations based on private storage,
independent source success, labels, scheduler state, or centralized
aggregation.

A pool is not reducible to:

- a list containing one row per contributor;
- separate mailboxes under one object name;
- a database table that a controller queries contributor by contributor;
- a global value assembled by a special controller from contributor-addressed
  records and supplied directly to the outcome path without a separately
  intervenable carrier state;
- two P2-I1-style histories that independently cause two later responses;
- co-occurrence without an auditable state/lineage handoff;
- a contributor label or count used as the response condition;
- semantic resource, collective memory, communication, or coordination; or
- a combined score with no independently reconstructible carrier state or
  active history.

One pool need not be spatially uniform, perfectly mixed, conserved, or equally
accessible to every possible participant. It must have one declared carrier
identity and access scope. Local gradients, order effects, saturation, or
bounded partitions may exist only when they are properties of that carrier and
do not become disguised private channels.

Reserve, accumulation, mixing, depletion, saturation, leakage, and maintenance
are not presumed to occur. Each must be measured or assigned an explicit reviewed
`not_applicable` rationale. An inapplicability disposition remains part of
the ecology-structure accounting; it is not permission to omit the question.

The contributors need not communicate, collaborate, or act simultaneously.
The later responder need not represent either source. The minimum ecological
claim concerns how several source histories become mutually relevant through
one shared condition.

## 5. Geometric meaning

The minimal geometric proposition is:

```text
source S1 at time t1 --perturbs--> shared carrier P
source S2 at time t2 --perturbs--> the same carrier P
                                  |
                                  v
               attributable combined state/history H_P(S1,S2[,order])
                                  |
                                  v
                 later opportunity at time t3 > max(t1,t2)
                                  |
                                  v
                    changed registered continuation response
```

The exact source carriers, pool carrier, perturbations, combination rule,
later response, and windows remain open until their declared decision gates.
They may be carried by source-current topology, support, capacity, flux,
causal-pulse surfaces, event history, or another audited PyGRC surface. An
explicitly constructed pool remains admissible only under the native-first and
construction-debt rules below.

For the relation to be geometrically meaningful:

1. Both source contributions must enter the same declared non-private carrier.
2. Each source must remain attributable without using private partitions as
   the claimed pool.
3. The combined carrier state or history must reconstruct independently of a
   success label or contributor-name lookup.
4. Writing, combination, and later encounter must be separated by native
   causal order or an explicitly controlled constructed transition.
5. A later response must depend on the combined mode-relevant carrier state,
   active history, or both under intervention.
6. Contributor deletion, label permutation, pooled-history shuffle, pool
   freeze, and direct/global substitution must distinguish the proposed cause
   from nearby alternatives.
7. Reserve, depletion, leakage, saturation, maintenance, support context, and
   producer ownership must be measured or explicitly classified.
8. The complete branch identity must include every native and ecology-owned
   state component that can affect continuation.

### 5.2 One-pool, causal-factorization, and access gate

“One pool” requires a declared identity boundary:

```text
carrier identity
access scope
state/history identity
source-lineage relation
write rule
read or susceptibility rule
persistence window
leakage boundary
```

The same object name, Python container, graph region, or serialized file does
not establish one pool. The realization must show which state is common and
which source-specific metadata is retained only for attribution. A later
response must consult or encounter the common state, not choose among private
source slots.

If safe attribution requires retaining source-local records, the brief does
not forbid them. The registered pool projection must demonstrate that those
records jointly determine one carrier condition and that the later response
does not bypass that condition by reading the records independently.

The minimum causal factorization is:

```text
common carrier transition:
  C_P(k+1) = U(C_P(k), q(k), local_context(k))

audit lineage:
  L(k+1) = audit_append(L(k), source_id(k), q(k), time(k))

later continuation:
  Y = V(C_P(t3), opportunity_context)

forbidden bypass:
  Y = G(L, contributor_slots, contributor_count, success_labels)
```

`C_P` contains encounter state, active carrier history, or both according to
the selected dependence mode. `q(k)` may include causal contribution
properties such as amount, location, timing, or registered contribution type.
An arbitrary source label may remain in `L`; it must not enter `V`. This
factorization constrains causal interfaces, not code location. A centrally
executed native transition or a carrier-owned scalar may qualify when the
carrier is independently intervenable and the response encounters it through
the declared carrier path. Centralized execution alone is not the forbidden
controller case.

The registration must make both directions falsifiable:

1. Hold the complete causal carrier state and active history fixed and alter
   attribution-only metadata. The later response must remain invariant under
   the frozen resolution/equality rule.
2. Hold attribution metadata, contributor activity, and unrelated opportunity
   conditions fixed while altering, blocking, or clamping the mode-relevant
   common carrier component. The registered response relation must change or
   the candidate effect must disappear. Otherwise combined-pool causal
   dependence is unsupported.

R01 also requires a non-private access witness. A later response must encounter
the carrier through its location, topology, or common eligibility, without a
source address. Access must follow the declared pool scope rather than
contributor identity. A responder may itself be a contributor, but its read or
susceptibility path must be the same carrier-scoped path available to another
eligible participant.

A retained audit or lineage log is not a functional pool merely because a
controller can query it. An event-history realization qualifies only when the
history is active runtime state encountered by the continuation mechanism and
is independently intervenable from its audit projection.

### 5.3 Combined-state or combined-history dependence gate

Joint dependence is functional rather than merely representational:

```text
combined carrier response
  differs from the registered nearest mode-appropriate primary comparator
  because the later opportunity encounters P_combined(t3), active H_P, or both
```

Mode-specific controls expected to be invariant do not become positive-margin
primary comparators. A state-preserving shuffle, for example, tests the
`state_carried` interpretation but cannot own a preregistered contrast that
requires divergence.

Both single-source responses must remain visible. They may be zero, partial,
aligned, counter-directional, or individually sufficient for a lower-level
effect. The lane does not require an AND gate or synergy. It requires evidence
that the claimed combined response is carried by one state/history whose
causal identity changes when contribution structure is altered.

A derived interaction, synergy, or order term may be retained as a secondary
diagnostic. It cannot replace the frozen primary normalized margin or the
causal controls.

Before calibration, the lane must select exactly one dependence mode:

```text
pool_dependence_mode:
  state_carried
  history_carried
  hybrid
```

The modes determine control semantics rather than claim strength:

- `state_carried`: the complete common state at encounter time is causal.
  Order or shuffle variants that preserve that state should preserve the later
  response. A discriminator must instead alter the state, prevent common
  constitution, partition the writes, or bypass the carrier.
- `history_carried`: ordered or temporally structured history is part of the
  carrier identity. A registered shuffle that preserves marginal quantities
  but changes that history should change the later response.
- `hybrid`: both encounter state and ordered history are causal. Registration
  must identify which interventions alter each component and separate
  state-matched history variants from history-matched state variants where the
  realization permits.

`hybrid` is admissible only when encounter state and active history are
separately declared, each has at least one intervention that changes it while
matching the other component as closely as the realization permits, and the
expected response relation for each intervention is frozen. Failure to obtain
the required separation closes lower or mixed; it cannot trigger a
post-outcome mode change.

The stable metric name `combined_pool_history_normalized_margin` does not force
a history-carried realization. For a state-carried realization, “history” in
that frozen identifier denotes the multi-contribution provenance of the
candidate state, not a requirement that the responder read temporal order.

At least one jointness counterfactual must preserve the inherited
single-source carrier relation while removing joint pool constitution. If the
registered combined consequence remains fully explained by that preserved
single-source relation, L02 cannot close as `supported_bounded_candidate`.

### 5.4 Attribution, order, and mixing gate

Attribution and pooling must coexist:

- source lineage identifies which perturbations entered the carrier;
- source-label permutation tests whether labels rather than source-relative
  contribution structure carry the response;
- at least two combined orders are frozen when order can matter;
- an order-invariant realization must justify why order is structurally
  inapplicable and still test a mode-appropriate state, constitution, or
  pooled-history discriminator;
- shuffling preserves registered marginal contribution quantity while
  producing the invariant or divergent relation declared by the selected
  dependence mode; and
- reconstruction verifies the complete source-to-pool-to-response chain.

“Mixing” means the registered rule by which contributions jointly affect the
carrier. It need not mean physical homogenization. The rule may preserve order,
locality, or source-relative structure, but it must not reduce the pool to a
dispatcher over private records.

### 5.5 Native, producer, and restoration gate

The realization search follows this order:

```text
source-current public PyGRC surface adequate
  -> use the native surface and retain its exact claim boundary

native surface present but inadequate for the L02 discriminator

     producer or ecology-owned state needed to expose the missing relation

```

A producer-backed pool is scientifically useful when it exposes a controlled
ecology relation and a precise graph-side demand. It remains producer-assisted
or constructed. If one producer creates the pool, reads it, and authors the
later success outcome without separable interventions, the result is
producer-carried and cannot satisfy the strong L02 boundary.

## 6. Observation model

The frozen primary metric is the L02 combined-pool-history normalized margin:

```text
combined_pool_history_response - matched_comparator_response
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
max(
  abs(combined_pool_history_response),
  abs(matched_comparator_response),
  measurement_resolution
)
```

The exact raw later-continuation response is not selected by this brief. It
must be chosen and justified before candidate-blind calibration, then remain
identical across calibration and live analysis. The response must be a
substrate-visible or explicitly bounded constructed consequence of encountering
the pool, not a post-hoc pool-presence label or authored success field.

The metric sheet permits a matched comparator drawn from the single-source,
shuffled-history, contributor-removal, or controller-exclusion structure. The
lane must freeze one exact primary candidate/comparator pairing before
calibration. Other comparisons remain mandatory causal diagnostics and cannot
be searched after execution for the largest margin.

The selected primary comparator must instantiate the closest registered
insufficient-repetition explanation for the chosen realization, not merely a
permitted comparator expected to yield a large margin. The preregistration
must state why it is the nearest causal alternative. If several alternatives
are comparably close, one remains primary and the others remain signed
mandatory controls; outcome magnitude cannot select among them.

The registration bundle must freeze an orientation transform before candidate
outcomes exist:

```text
oriented response =  raw response   when higher is the aligned direction
oriented response = -raw response   when lower is the aligned direction
```

Per-seed margins and every raw single-source, combined-order, shuffle, label,
removal, freeze/clamp, private-partition, controller-exclusion,
quantity-matched, and transfer-contrast response remain visible.
The strict zero threshold and candidate-blind resolution band describe
direction and resolution; they do not decide the terminal class. A robust
margin can still be mailbox-carried, controller-produced, leakage-dependent,
or fixture-locked. A narrow, mixed, or counter-directional relation may still
disclose a useful pool boundary or missing native distinction.

The primary claim is distributional. A single combined event or one successful
order cannot establish a shared-pool possibility structure.

## 9. Anti-subsumption boundary

L02 owns multi-source constitution of one functional pool. It must not absorb
another lane merely because that lane also uses several activities or shared
state.

| Other lane | Defining causal distinction | When an L02 observation should redirect rather than subsume |
| --- | --- | --- |
| L01 — niche conditioning | One attributable history differentially alters later participant-relative possibility | The result is fully explained by one source history or by reader susceptibility rather than joint pool constitution. |
| L03 — trail/stigmergic field | Costly repeated deposition changes decay, reinforcement, saturation, or maintenance dynamics that alter traversal | Trace dynamics and route function, rather than multi-source combined state, carry the result. |
| L04 — nursery/support field | Produced, depleted, or maintained support differentially governs fragile versus robust local formation | Support provisioning and fragility are the causal center. |
| L05 — boundary-conditioned exchange | Maintained selective coupling controls exchange, transformation, leakage, repair, and later closure | Interface state and permeability carry the effect. |
| L06 — capacity circulation | An audited depletion/replenishment or return cycle closes a capacity budget and preserves floors | Circulation and budget closure, not pool constitution, explain the later response. |
| L07 — parent-basin modulation demand | A parent/local causal distinction is required but absent or unsuitable | The effect depends on parent-wide modulation, copied parameters, or a missing graph distinction. |

Multiple participants responding to one pool may be recorded as an adjacent
observation, but L02 cannot call that coordination or co-response without a
separately controlled relation and a permissible claim ceiling. A redirect
does not erase the L02 observation or move its evidence into another lane. It
becomes a bounded next move or a P2-I8 synthesis input with independent lineage
preserved.
