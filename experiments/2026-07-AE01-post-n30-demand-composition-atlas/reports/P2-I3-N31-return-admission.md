## 3. What N31 established

N31 separates several meanings that P2-I3 had provisionally grouped under
“decay.” This classification is more important than finding the largest
effect.

### 3.1 Ordinary coherence evolution

N31 found an exact native representation for the D0a route projection and
native formation/persistence at `DR2`. It did not find an ordinary autonomous
weakening trajectory in the executed fixtures. Therefore:

```text
native coherence organization exists
!= ordinary autonomous decay exists
```

`D0-R` remains not instantiated in the tested domain and is not globally
refuted. B-R must not be relabeled as D0-R merely because both conserve global
coherence.

### 3.2 Derived observables

The finite-window D0b relation reaches `DR3` as a fading observable but does
not mediate the later readout. D0c remains an instantaneous geometric
comparator at `DR1`. Both are useful measurement or diagnostic surfaces;
neither supplies P2-I3's causal non-static field dynamic.

### 3.3 Release attenuation

Candidate A reaches a producer-mediated `DR5` boundary for release expression.
It was not rejected as a failed mechanism. It was not issued a current return
contract because P2-I3's demand concerns field state rather than attenuation
of what a source releases. RCAE could revisit it only by explicitly changing
that demand.

### 3.4 B-R: conservative redistribution

B-R reaches `DR5_producer_mediated` and receives the reusable contract
`n31_B_R_conserved_redistribution_contract_v2` at
`DR6_contract_only`.

Its exact causal organization is:

```text
registered native route contrast forms and persists
-> declared producer policy selects export eligibility, amount, time,
   and explicit destination
-> native LGRC9V3 packet transport debits source and credits destination
-> local route mass and contrast weaken by an attributable amount
-> later local source-C admission changes
```

The accounting invariant is exact:

```text
source debit
= in-flight packet amount
= destination credit
```

The destination must be outside the readout path. Source-C clamping reverses
the readout while destination-C clamping does not, establishing a bounded
partial local mediation result. Full route-distribution mediation and an
ordinary D0-R bridge remain debt.

### 3.5 C.2: exact history-derived susceptibility

C.2 establishes an exact relation derived from serialized native packet
history, reaches `DR5_producer_extension`, and receives the reusable contract
`n31_C2_exact_history_susceptibility_contract_v2` at
`DR6_contract_only`.

Its relation has the N31 form:

```text
S_e = S_floor
      + clip(sum(alpha * q_r * rho^delta_tau),
```


This is activity-indexed rather than wall-clock decay. Progression occurs
through qualifying committed local arrivals in the native event history. The
history is native and the relation is exactly re-derivable, but LGRC9V3 has no
native constitutive hook that inserts the derived susceptibility into route

## 4. Native status is a claim boundary, not an admission preference

Many graph N-series experiments validly use producer-mediated mechanisms.
P2-I3 therefore does not rank B-R or C.2 by how native they are. The relevant
selection questions are:

- does the mechanism express the ecology relation being sought;
- is its carrier and topology appropriate;
- can later traversal locally encounter it;
- are its state, time, conservation, and restoration boundaries honest; and
- can its producer residue be controlled without authoring the outcome?

Nativity records implementation ownership, claim ceiling, and
naturalization debt. It does not make a producer-mediated result defective,
nor may a nearby native operation replace a more semantically adequate
constructed mechanism.

## 6. The bounded distribution-metadata error

The retained N31 source-authority record reports:

```text

```

The exact source-anchor `pyproject.toml` declares `0.1`, and installing that
source into the graph `.venv` yields `pygrc==0.1`. The editable import resolves
directly to the admitted graph source. Reconstruction at the exact source
anchor passes every builder check but produces the source-declared version.
The project owner therefore classifies the retained `0.0.0` value as an
incorrect installed-distribution metadata value, not as a runtime-source or
provider-contract discrepancy.

The validator proves a narrow envelope:

| Reconstruction class | Count | Content |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| byte-exact | 5 | B-R contract, C.2 contract, candidate matrix, claim/debt register, RCAE recommendation |
| bounded difference | 3 | source-authority record, top-level closeout, trace |

Every bounded difference is exactly one of:

- `pygrc_version`; or
- an output digest/SHA field that depends on the changed source-authority
  bytes.

There is no scientific-result, provider-contract, candidate-disposition, or
recommendation difference. The metadata error remains visible as historical
reconstruction debt, but it does not justify discarding or silently repairing
the N31 contracts.

This return transition rebuilds I12 from the exact retained I1/I2/I8/I10/I11
inputs and verifies all eleven identities. It does not reexecute the upstream
