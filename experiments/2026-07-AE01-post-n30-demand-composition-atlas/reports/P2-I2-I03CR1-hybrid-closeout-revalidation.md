## 3. Exact hybrid carrier and component causality

The retained carrier is one bound common composite:

```text
P + authoritative H_P + M_H = R_H(H_P) + [P,M_H]/[B_ref] -> native V
```

`L` remains separate attribution/audit lineage. P, H_P, M_H, the mask, the
adapter, a feedback row, or a query/controller object cannot substitute for
the composite carrier.

P and H_P are independently causal through the same native V. The P-only
debit retains H_P tokens and M_H while changing P and the raw native score.
The H-only clamp replaces authoritative H_P, rematerializes M_H through native
packets, retains P, and changes the same score. The adapter readout does not
read P, and neither intervention alters the response configuration or masks.
The architectural claim is therefore stronger than a two-node state-only
description, but it does not assert irreducible history, non-Markovianity,
nonlinearity, interaction, synergy, or superadditivity.

H_P remains authoritative. M_H is only its declared native output port and
must equal the registered `R_H(H_P)` before V; disagreement fails closed.
Pre-response, clamp/order, save/load, reset, and equal-input continuation
identities retain this relation.
