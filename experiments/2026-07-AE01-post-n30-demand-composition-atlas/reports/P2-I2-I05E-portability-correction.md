## Historical-to-portable boundary

metadata identifies the historical commit and source digest. The historical

claim hashes. The current claim projection remains at the governed claim path,
is read-only, and still permanently blocks a second start.

The projection validator reconstructs the four changed JSON records from their
historical Git blobs using only the frozen field transformations. Every other
field must remain equal. It also verifies the raw final receipt, one attempt,
one builder call, zero retries, one readback, zero reconstruction generations,
authorization consumption, second-start refusal, candidate exclusion, and the
unchanged arithmetic floor and delta.
