## Consequence for APP-B2 and Appendix A

APP-B2 established that GEP responds while reference, G, E, P, GE, GP, and EP
are zero in the history-carried realization. APP-B4 shows why that proper-
subset contrast must not be promoted to distinct-operation complementarity:

- repeated `PPP` responds;
- repeated/mixed `GGP`, `GPP`, `EPP`, `PGP`, and `PEP` also respond;
- GEP is one member of the broader terminal-P response class; and
- valid three-token sequences without terminal P are zero.

Taken together, the retained evidence supports a **three-admission,
terminal-P-qualified history gate** within the frozen scaffold. A single P and
the tested one-/two-operation proper subsets do not suffice, but physically
distinct G, E, and P identities are not necessary.

This sharpens the difference from Appendix A. Appendix A retains bounded
causal evidence that its distinct generative, extractive, and redistributive
operations are jointly necessary in a different live four-axis carrier.
Appendix B's P2-I2 history projection instead realizes an order- and
quantity-sensitive temporal gate whose binary decision collapses to terminal
P over the tested three-token domain. This is a useful mechanism, but not the
same mechanism or claim.

## Functionality and possible foundations

The bounded functionality demonstrated by APP-B4 is:

- source-label-free ordered event admission;
- quantitative recency folding through a shared history carrier;
- deterministic selection of one terminal-token class after a filled
  three-admission context;
- native threshold-to-packet conversion and later B response; and
- save/load/reset-valid execution in fresh PyGRC models.

With further interventions and transfer tests, this could ground temporal
eligibility windows, last-event-qualified triggers, bounded sequence filters,
or carrier-native finite-history policies. Those are possible foundations,
not semantic claims that APP-B4 itself establishes.

APP-B4 does not establish communication, cooperation, coordination, symbolic
recognition, general sequence learning, ecological recurrence, or native
active-history support in PyGRC. The history carrier remains scaffold-
dependent, and graph-native implementation priority still requires
cross-experiment synthesis.
