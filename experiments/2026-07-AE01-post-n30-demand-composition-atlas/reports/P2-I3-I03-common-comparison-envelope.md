# P2-I3 I03 Common Comparison Envelope

**Status:** accepted prospective contract

**Iteration:** `P2-I3-I03`

**Lane:** `AE01-L03`

**Controlling decision:** accepted `P2-I3-DEC-030`, under accepted
`P2-I3-DEC-026`

**Evidence effect:** comparison semantics only. This envelope supplies no
realization acceptance, calibration value, conformance result, candidate
execution, branch result, provider selection, or lane result.

## 1. Why this envelope exists

P2-I3 will investigate two distinct N31-returned provider ontologies:

```text
B-R  conservative redistribution and route-local source weakening
C.2  exact-history susceptibility closure
```

They cannot be made comparable by forcing them into one topology, equation,
runtime adapter, numeric metric, or scalar implementation-cost score. Doing so
would erase the very difference the staged program is meant to examine.

They can be compared by asking the same ecology questions of each separately:

```text
Did costly repeated activity create a non-static route-local aftereffect?
Did a later local opportunity encounter it?
Did intervention establish that it changed continuation?
Which controls, perturbations, and variations survived?
What claim and developmental value follow?
Which producer residue was causally required?
```

The machine-readable authority is
[the common comparison envelope](../contracts/p2-i3/i03-common-comparison-envelope.json).

## 2. Comparison is semantic, not numerically symmetric

Each branch retains its own causal realization, metric, calibration,
registration, execution, controls, and interpretation. The common envelope
does not presume that a B-R source-coherence margin and a future C.2
susceptibility response have commensurable magnitudes.

Cross-branch comparison therefore operates first on:

- validity and reconstruction;
- the same eight L03 scientific questions;
- the frozen R01–R05 ladder;
- relation and control dispositions;
- stability, perturbation, and variation evidence;
- state/history classification;
- failure, missing-surface, and redirect information;
- claim ceiling and developmental value; and
- an unsummed producer-cost vector.

Raw effect sizes remain visible within each branch. They may be compared
numerically across branches only if a later prospective record demonstrates
semantic equivalence of response, units, timing, comparator, aggregation,
missingness, and calibration population. Convenience or common normalization
is not equivalence.

Thresholds remain reference surfaces. A same-side movement, narrow split,
counter-directional result, or missing binary transition must be interpreted;
none is erased by a universal pass/fail gate.

## 3. The eight common scientific axes

| Axis | Common question | Boundary protected |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `SCI-01` | Did at least two attributable costly events form the route-local aftereffect? | Static fixture state or free installation is not formation. |
| `SCI-02` | Did a registered non-static dynamic occur beyond persistence? | Replaying N30's persistent trace is insufficient for L03. |
| `SCI-03` | Did a later local opportunity encounter the field without hidden selection? | A global ranker, label reader, or outcome-writing producer cannot carry the result. |
| `SCI-04` | Did intervention on the dynamic or mediator change continuation? | Correlation or co-occurrence is below the causal boundary. |
| `SCI-05` | Do withdrawal, shuffle/relocation, false-trace, and no-field controls establish specificity? | Generic route, quantity, and fixture effects remain separate. |
| `SCI-06` | Is continuation carried by complete current state, active history, or an unresolved distinction? | Audit history is not causal history; incomplete state matching proves neither. |
| `SCI-07` | How stable, perturbation-sensitive, narrow, transferable, or regime-limited is the relation? | One favorable registered point is not general ecological strength. |
| `SCI-08` | What R01–R05 rung, claim ceiling, developmental value, and next move are honest? | A mechanism result cannot inherit stronger ecological vocabulary automatically. |

These are mandatory questions, not mandatory positive answers. A valid branch
may close with a static trace, generic quantity effect, unresolved state match,
missing native surface, producer dependence, narrow relation, redirect, or
non-selection. Infrastructure failure remains separate from scientific
negative evidence.

## 4. Producer cost remains a vector

Every producer item is classified as:

```text
contract_required
  required by the admitted N31 provider contract

rcae_ecology_required
  additionally required to compose the L03 topology or encounter

evidence_only
  required to test, observe, control, reconstruct, or audit the mechanism
```

The classes are mutually exclusive by precedence: provider-contract duty
first, additional ecology-composition duty second, and non-causal evidence
work third. Reuse of a contract-required operation by the ecology composition
does not move it into the second class or count it twice.

Within those classes, each branch reports six dimensions:

1. causal operations and invocation counts;
2. causal state, topology, schedules, and serialized lifecycle;
3. producer-specific controls, failures, omission dependence, and refusal
   guards;
4. restoration, cross-load protection, replay, and reconstruction burden;
5. descriptive source/configuration, state/artifact, runtime, memory, disk,
   and process footprint; and
6. naturalization debt and the exact graph-side surface that would remove it.

The dimensions are not summed. Source lines, bytes, wall time, or invocation
counts cannot decide scientific adequacy. Evidence-only rigor is not mechanism
cost, and a producer-mediated result is not defective merely because a native
surface is absent. Conversely, a strong result does not erase causal producer
dependence.

## 5. Sequential development is explicit

B-R executes before C.2 because DEC-026 selected a developmental sequence, not
a blind symmetric competition. Before C.2 calibration, P2-I3 must retain a
prospective influence register for every B-R-derived input to C.2 design:

```text
source B-R observation or artifact
-> adopted / rejected / not applicable
-> prospective C.2 design effect and reason
-> comparability or independence consequence
```

The register may improve the second experiment. It may not import B-R
candidate values as C.2 calibration, retune C.2 to outperform B-R, or silently
reuse B-R topology, carrier, equation, adapter, or threshold. The final report
must call the result a sequential developmental comparison.

## 6. What this freezes and defers

This envelope freezes:

- common comparison questions and statuses;
- required branch-closeout inputs;
- the separation of scientific adequacy from producer burden;
- the producer-cost classes and dimensions;
- the C.2 influence-register obligation; and
- the non-scalar, non-automatic Q-021 decision boundary.

It deliberately defers:

- Q-013 quantity-matched and complete-state-matched identities;
- Q-015 restoration and equal-continuation design;
- branch-specific metrics, comparators, and resolution bands;
- exact topology, values, schedules, times, seeds, and attempts;
- every conformance and scientific outcome; and
- provider retention or composition selection.

## 7. Q-021 boundary

Only after both branch closeouts may Q-021 retain B-R, retain C.2, retain both
as alternatives, propose a separately governed composition, retain another
justified route, or select none. No field in this envelope computes a winner.
The later decision must reason explicitly from scientific adequacy, mechanism
meaning, producer burden, naturalization path, failure information, and
developmental value.

## 8. Reopening conditions

Reopen the envelope if it privileges one provider ontology instead of L03's
causal question; cannot represent a branch without erasing its mechanism;
mixes evidence rigor with causal residue; requires a numeric cross-branch
comparison without demonstrated equivalence; cannot preserve prospective C.2
influence provenance; or new graph evidence changes a provider contract.

A surprising, weak, narrow, or negative branch result does not itself reopen
the envelope. It is interpreted inside it.
