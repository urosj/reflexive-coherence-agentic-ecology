## 1. Role and core question

The Post-N30 Agentic-Ecology Demand and Composition Atlas is a bounded local
experiment with specification-like contracts. It is not a canonical
specification, a reusable ecology runtime, or graph experiment N31.

Its core question is:

> Which ecology-side shared-medium patterns can be consumed, composed, or
> explicitly constructed from the admitted N29/N30 handoff; where do those
> compositions fail, interfere, or expose missing substrate distinctions; and
> which recurring demands, if any, justify proposing one later graph-side
> primitive or building-block experiment?

The atlas is allowed to conclude that no candidate is ready. It is not designed
to force an N31+ selection or to demonstrate agency, semantic coordination, or
an ecology regime.

## 2. Graph/ecology spiral and project boundary

The graph project owns LGRC runtime implementation, source-current substrate
evidence, graph experiment naming, native-mechanism admission, and graph-side
claim ceilings. RCAE owns ecology-side demand discovery, controlled
composition, constructed demand probes, domain-shaped interpretation, and the
return of precise missing-surface contracts.

```text
N29 bridge/debt/probe-contract handoff
  -> N30 P2/M2 trace-mediated eligibility closeout
  -> AE01 demand and composition atlas
  -> selected or non-selected N31+ demand contract
  -> separately authorized graph-side experiment
  -> later explicit RCAE consumption profile
```

RCAE treats the graph/PyGRC repository as read-only. No roadmap, adapter,
experiment, test, script, reconstruction command, or promotion step governed
here may modify it. A native PyGRC realization is graph-owned work outside AE01.

Demand is not substrate evidence. Constructed RCAE behavior remains
constructed even if it exposes a useful future LGRC distinction. A later native
surface never retroactively upgrades an earlier constructed run.

## 5. Catalog and domain rules

The catalog hierarchy is:

```text
primitive -> building block -> ecology motif -> ecology regime
```

- A primitive is a minimal substrate-visible capacity, relation, carrier
  surface, or distinguishable state change.
- A building block combines primitives through a declared dynamics generator
  and composition interface.
- A motif is a domain-shaped controlled composition.
- A regime is a persistent parent-basin organization with cross-time
  continuity, turnover, perturbation, and recovery evidence.

Every pattern card declares exactly one primary catalog layer. Secondary
observations may be recorded but cannot promote the result. The primary layer
is chosen from the operational question and required evidence, not from the
lane name or an anticipated positive result.

Domain role is a separate axis. A domain may be absent, illustrative, a test
fixture, an interpretation context, or a later integrated candidate. RC-Ant's
conceptual prominence does not select an ant package or make an ant-shaped
fixture evidence of a motif or regime. No domain package is selected in AE01.

## 6. Shared-medium and parent-basin ontology requirements

Every applicable lane must define:

```text
participant or local differentiation
participant continuity or identity criterion
non-private medium carrier and access scope
participant-caused perturbation and provenance
source-current or explicitly constructed surface change
trace persistence, decay, reinforcement, saturation, and cost
later eligibility, susceptibility, formation, or continuation dependency
relation-chain lineage and causal order
parent-basin boundary, persistence condition, reserve, and closure relevance
participant/medium separation or co-constitution account
```

N30 supports only the bounded relation through trace-conditioned later
eligibility at P2/M2. Parent-basin relevance, co-response, niche, support field,
pool, trail, and circulation are ecology-side questions unless a lane identifies
a narrower inherited source leg.

A parent basin is the declared persistence and support context within which
local differentiations become mutually relevant. It is not automatically a
collective agent, global controller, semantic goal, organism, colony identity,
or ecology regime. A lane that cannot identify a parent-basin account must
classify that absence rather than silently assuming a passive environment.

## 8. Lane independence, execution, and rename rules

All seven lanes may execute independently after P1-GATE. None may consume
another lane's conclusion as evidence. Shared infrastructure, source records,
or constructed mechanisms may be referenced, but every lane must preserve its
own inputs, controls, result lineage, and claim ceiling.

Cross-lane recurrence and composition synthesis begin only after every lane has
a valid terminal record. A lane is retained even when it produces no positive
candidate.

A display name may change only during controlled contract revision to improve
ontology or remove a premature claim. A rename must preserve:

- stable lane ID;
- initial and previous display names;
- controlling contract revision;
- rationale and affected projections;
- unchanged or explicitly revised scope.

P1-I3 freezes the machine-readable registry. P1-I5 must fail when the plan,
roadmap, AE01 README, or registry has missing, duplicate, stale, or reordered
lane identities.

## 9. Debt taxonomy

Every applicable record carries these general debt axes:

| Debt | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Producer debt | An external producer still carries desired dynamics, routing, or transitions. |
| Medium debt | Shared relation depends on a message, global state, free trace, or unsupported leakage policy. |
| Naturalization debt | A constructed or inherited mechanism has not been reproduced as source-current native substrate behavior. |
| Semantic debt | Interpretation exceeds substrate-visible evidence or imports goal, meaning, cooperation, care, or agency. |
| Transfer debt | A result is fixture-bound or lacks meaningful variants. |
| Composition debt | Component interaction, ordered handoff, compatible timescale, budget, or combined controls remain untested. |
| Measurement debt | A required source-current signature or stable discriminator is absent or ambiguous. |
| Claim debt | Evidence cannot support a desired label or catalog promotion. |

The six canonical N29 debts remain mandatory named rows:

```text
composed_ecology_runtime_harness_missing
producer_mediated_cross_prototype_handoff
medium_debt_and_nonzero_leakage_policy
ap4_ap5_gap_propagation
resource_economy_cooperation_exploitation_semantics
deviation_nativity_discharge
```

No lane may silently discharge them. A non-applicable disposition requires an
explicit rationale; a discharge requires newly admitted dedicated evidence.

## 10. Failure and terminal taxonomy

P1-I4 will freeze detailed failure signatures. P1-I2 fixes the top-level
terminal vocabulary:

| Terminal classification | Meaning | Lane-gate effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `supported_bounded_candidate` | Frozen positive signatures and required controls support a result within the lane ceiling. | May close the lane; does not imply transfer, composition, motif, regime, or N31 selection. |
| `partial_or_mixed` | Some required distinctions are supported while others fail, remain producer-carried, or conflict. | May close with explicit supported and unsupported legs. |
| `not_supported` | A valid completed probe fails its frozen positive signatures or supports its declared null. | May close as negative scientific evidence. |
| `blocked_missing_prerequisite` | A required surface, carrier, runtime capability, or discriminator is absent or unsuitable. | May close as demand/missing-surface evidence, not as proof of the proposed mechanism. |
| `incomplete_execution` | Execution or validation could not complete, so neither positive nor negative scientific classification is justified. | May close only through a complete incompleteness record; contributes no support/refutation and forces N31+ non-selection. |

A complete terminal record must contain its stopping condition, attempted work,
controls reached or not reached, reconstruction status, missing information,
debts, and claim impact. `incomplete_execution` must not be disguised as
`not_supported`, used in recurrence scoring, or kept indefinitely open.

Detailed failure subtypes include no primitive, unstable primitive,
producer-carried only, medium absent, proxy success, fixture lock, composition
interference, claim inflation, regime fragmentation, and dormancy ambiguity.

## 11. Evidence-use and construction boundary

AE01 uses:

```text
exploratory_scratch -> registered_probe -> retained_evidence
```

Scratch may inform contract design but cannot support a classification or gate.
A registered probe has a frozen question, controls, stopping condition, and
execution mode. Only verified retained evidence can support a report fact,
terminal classification, or gate.

Constructed mechanisms are allowed when an admitted LGRC surface is missing or
creates a composition tension. Every construction must state necessity,
minimality, inputs/outputs, counterfactual, scaffold withdrawal, debts, claim
ceiling, and proposed graph discriminator. Shared AE01 construction code may
remain experiment-local. Placement or repeated use does not make it native or
admitted.

## 16. Stopping, closeout, and non-selection

A lane stops when one frozen terminal classification has a complete closure
record. A lane must not be removed because its result is negative, blocked, or
incomplete.

AE01 must close with explicit N31+ non-selection when:

- no candidate passes every eligibility gate and threshold;
- a tie remains after the declared procedure;
- sensitivity profiles change the winner;
- any required lane ends as `incomplete_execution`;
- conceptual demand is the primary support;
- reconstruction cannot be bounded; or
- an unnamed stronger prerequisite is required.

Program closeout may report a lower supported rung if later gates cannot pass.
It must preserve incomplete work and remaining debt rather than forcing C6.

## 17. N31+ ranking boundary

Only eligible candidates after cross-lane synthesis receive D-029 scores. A
candidate may originate as:

```text
consume | extend | introduce
```

Scoring uses the frozen ten dimensions, non-compensable thresholds, tie band,
tie order, and four sensitivity profiles in the master plan. Every candidate
also keeps an independent qualitative mechanism rationale and any declared
investigator-prior ordering. Disagreement is reported; intuition cannot silently
override a gate or score.

Selection is ecology-side guidance only. The graph project may accept, reject,
rename, reorder, or replace the proposed experiment and retains all graph-side
implementation and evidence authority.
