- Completed and owner-accepted AE01 L01/P2-I1. The bounded result shows that
  the same attributable shared-medium history creates different later
  formation possibilities across matched susceptibility configurations. The
  result is classified as a niche-conditioning demand pattern, not autonomous
  niche formation. See the [P2-I1 C02
  result](experiments/2026-07-AE01-post-n30-demand-composition-atlas/reports/P2-I1-C02-result.md).

  evaluable terminals across state-carried, history-carried, and hybrid
  realizations. The result supports bounded shared-pool co-conditioning at a
  scaffold-dependent `T3_operational_class` ceiling, with native state-carried
  expression and one recurring active-history adapter debt. See the [P2-I2 I11
  closeout](experiments/2026-07-AE01-post-n30-demand-composition-atlas/reports/P2-I2-I11-terminal-closeout.md).
- Completed P2-I2 Appendix A with 19/19 valid fresh-runtime arms and 53/53
  closeout checks. Generator, extractor, and redistributor operations jointly
  place a live multi-axis carrier inside the registered functional envelope,
  while every proper subset is insufficient. The result supports bounded
  fixture-level operation complementarity, not metabolism or organization. See
  the [Appendix A
  closeout](experiments/2026-07-AE01-post-n30-demand-composition-atlas/reports/P2-I2-APP-A2-fresh-runtime-matrix-and-closeout.md).
- Completed P2-I2 Appendix B/APP-B4 with 75/75 valid fresh PyGRC arms. Across
  all executable three-token triples and registered seeds, response occurs if
  and only if the terminal token is P. This distinguishes a three-admission
  terminal-P quantitative-history gate from Appendix A operation
  complementarity. See the [APP-B4
  result](experiments/2026-07-AE01-post-n30-demand-composition-atlas/reports/P2-I2-APP-B4-runtime-result.md).
- Retained restoration, continuation, reset, causal-receipt, control, and
  byte-identical reconstruction evidence for the accepted main P2-I2 result,
  together with causal-control and reconstruction evidence for its appendices.
